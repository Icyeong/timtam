Step 2
In a blender combine milk, raspberries and ice cream; blend until smooth and creamy.

Step 3
Stir in the Tim Tam crumbs, roughly chop remaining Tim Tam biscuits and use for garnish. Serve immediately.

Step 2

Step 3

Step 4
Refrigerate for 30 minutes to set.
